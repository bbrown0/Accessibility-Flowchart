Accessibility Teams in PDLC

Decision flowchart for when to engage DAC, ACIS, and WAT teams

1
Planning & Strategy Phase
Primary lead - LEAD
DAC
Digital Accessibility Compliance
Policy development, standards setting, risk assessment
Setting accessibility requirements, defining standards, conducting risk analysis, establishing corporate policy
Consulting role - CONSULT
ACIS
Accessible Content and Inclusive Solutions
Content strategy planning for inclusive design
Planning content creation workflows, defining inclusive authoring processes, early content strategy consultation
Not involved - MINIMAL
WAT
Web Accessibility Testing
Not typically involved in planning phase
Limited involvement at this stage - may provide input on testing requirements for planning purposes
2
Design & Architecture Phase
Secondary support - SUPPORT
DAC
Digital Accessibility Compliance
Technical standards guidance, universal design principles
WCAG/WAI-ARIA expertise needed, innovative solutions required, complex accessibility challenges, AI/ML integration
Primary lead - LEAD
ACIS
Accessible Content and Inclusive Solutions
Accessible task design, inclusive content authoring solutions
Designing content creation processes, assessment tasks, authoring workflows, inclusive design implementation
Consulting role - CONSULT
WAT
Web Accessibility Testing
Testing methodology consultation
Understanding testing requirements for design decisions, early testability review, QA process planning
3
Development & Content Creation Phase
Consulting role - CONSULT
DAC
Digital Accessibility Compliance
Custom solutions, remediation guidance
Complex technical issues, custom engineering needs, AI/ML integration, innovative solution development
Primary lead - LEAD
ACIS
Accessible Content and Inclusive Solutions
Accessible content creation, alt text, alternate formats
Creating base content, supplemental accessible content, alternate test formats (large print, braille), content authoring
Secondary support - SUPPORT
WAT
Web Accessibility Testing
Early accessibility validation
Development checkpoints requiring accessibility validation, iterative testing during development
4
Testing & Quality Assurance Phase
Consulting role - CONSULT
DAC
Digital Accessibility Compliance
Usability analysis, complex issue resolution
Accessibility usability testing needed, resolving complex conformance issues, user experience analysis
Secondary support - SUPPORT
ACIS
Accessible Content and Inclusive Solutions
Content-related testing support
Testing reveals content accessibility issues requiring remediation, content validation and correction
Primary lead - LEAD
WAT
Web Accessibility Testing
End-to-end WCAG conformance testing
Comprehensive accessibility testing of applications, assessments, products - automated and manual testing methodologies
5
Deployment & Monitoring Phase
Primary lead - LEAD
DAC
Digital Accessibility Compliance
Risk management, policy compliance, ongoing monitoring
Ensuring corporate policy compliance, managing accessibility risks, post-launch monitoring, regulatory compliance
Consulting role - CONSULT
ACIS
Accessible Content and Inclusive Solutions
Content updates and maintenance
Ongoing content accessibility maintenance, content updates required, new accessible format needs
Secondary support - SUPPORT
WAT
Web Accessibility Testing
Final verification and periodic re-testing
Final pre-launch verification, scheduled accessibility audits, ongoing compliance testing

Team Engagement Levels

★
LEAD - Primary Responsibility Main decision-making authority and accountability for accessibility outcomes in this phase
▲
SUPPORT - Secondary Role Active supporting role with specific expertise contributions and collaborative involvement
◆
CONSULT - Advisory Role Available for consultation when specialized expertise is needed, on-demand involvement
○
MINIMAL - Limited Involvement Little to no involvement in this phase, may provide limited input if specifically requested
